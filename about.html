About MetricMover

Hello, I MetricMover, a Site Reliability Engineer and Cloud Architecture student. By day I work at a large Fortune 20 bank in their cybersecurity department, ensuring the application security scanners stay up and running 24/7 with three "nines" of availability (99.9% availability) .By night I attend a very large university where I am enrolled in their ABET accredited Cloud Computing program. I spend a lot of time at work writing scrapers and APIs to move custom server and application metrics from highly-distributed systems to fault-tolerant datastores.

For fun I generally like to play video games, especially any single-player or couch co-op games from Nintendo, or massive-world RPGs such as Fallout. I have a gorgeous wife, a silly Labrador, and two mischevious little kitties.

On this blog you will find various write-ups and tutorials of custom metric moving and general Systems/Site Reliability Engineering stuff. I hope you enjoy your time here!
